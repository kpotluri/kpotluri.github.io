Feasibility Study of a Knowledge Management System

This project is about conducting a research on the feasibility of a knowledge management system in an organization. The research focusses on understanding the motivations and barriers that affect the usability of a knowledge management system in an organization.

Review Digest

In a world of increasing online markets, it becomes extremeley painful for users to research about the items they wish to purchase. The process involves digging through thousands and thousands of reviews from other people that are biased or influenced in some or the other way. The Review Digest aims at utlizing text processing to go through these thousands of reviews and consolidate them for the end user in a short summary, that talks about all the pros and cons about the product.

FatNLazy

A Web App, that tells you about all the food joints that serve the type of food you want, in the area you want. And if you want, it can also book an Uber to take you there!!!
